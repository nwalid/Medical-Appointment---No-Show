Introduction¶

The aim of this report will be analyzing and drawing conclusions around a healthcare dataset related to Dr Appointment and No show from patients. Firtly I will load libraries and the data set, second I will wrangle and analyse the data to help us answer the below questions:

  • Q1: Does certain neighbourhood have higher No show appointment rate?
  • Q2: Do people financial background has a diret effect on No show appointment?
  • Q3: what effect the duration between scheduling an appointment and the actual appointment have on No show?
  • Q4: Do certain known medical conditions have higher No show appointment rates than others?

Conclusions¶

Results : Our data suggest¶

1- Two (2) neighbourhood account to about 14,000 missing appointments alone. This need to be carefully futher study and additional data on those neighbourhood is required. The available data is limited to the location of the Healthcare centre for the appointments and available infrastructure.

2-Scholarship, where people are recipient of governmental social support did not show any correlation for higher rate of noshow.

3-High duration between date of when the appointment was booked and actual appointment date has contribution to show. Duration should be kept below 27 days.

4-Diabetes, Alcoholism, Hypertension and Handycap did not represent any direct correlation with the noshow.

5- SMS Received play a significant contribution to the noshopw based on this data set. Patient who did not receive SMS are more likely to miss their appointment and become now show.

Limitations: There are couple of Limitations in our data¶

1- Neighbourhood Location require deeper analysis and dataset to establish the geographical characteristics of those Neighbourhood where noshow is highest

2- Sms_received does not specify when the sms what sent and therefore cannt draw any relation between day of Sms_received and AppointmentDay
